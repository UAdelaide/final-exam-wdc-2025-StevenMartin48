Dog Walk Service

This synergistic progressive webapp with blockchain and AI integration will be the web2.0 upgrade your unsynergised dog's walking life. This is ultimately a lifestyle app with integrated social media features. Through this application it is made possible for clients to establish and maintain a level of integration of one's dog's walking needs with the need of people who desire to walk dogs in exchange for compensation. Through this we are able to synergise one's needs with that of another party
